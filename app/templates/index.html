About me

Hello, I'm Kevin! I'm currently studying Computer Science at Universidad Federico Santa María in Valparaíso, Chile. Without a doubt, one of the best decisions I have made in my life is to enter to study this career, I love it more everyday. There were many areas that interested me, however, the one that touched my heart was Data Science, I feel a great satisfaction when I see that an intangible good is contributing so much to social growth.
Another thing that caught my attention was the transversality of this area of knowledge, about the latter I usually play music, my favorite musical instrument is the saxophone, I enjoy feeling that my body is one with it, I feel the music when I play it. Nevertheless, in the last two years I have been practicing quite a bit of fingerstyle guitar and piano. A few years ago I invested one year of self-study in music theory, and since then I have put the theory into practice in the 3 instruments, I usually reuse the licks that I have created in one in the rest of the instruments, lately I have been using the scale of blues and some greek modes. My favorite styles of music are classical and jazz, I really enjoy listening to Debussy when I work or study. Generally, in my social networks I follow popularizers of a variety of topics such as, technology, medicine, astronomy, economy, neuroscience, biology, music, politics, physics, math and in general, science. I enjoy learning new things all the time, and everytime I explore a new topic I see multiple possible applications of data science.
As you have seen on the website I associate data science with what a detective does, looking at small details and patterns can add great value to complex problem solving, we certainly do not have the super skills of Sherlock Holmes, but if we ask the right questions with the help of a good algorithm we can find those little details that answer big questions!
Finally, I've been learning english for 3 years, I've decided to write everything in this portfolio in English to test my skills, in advance I apologize for my possible mistakes.

My Proffesional Interests

I want to be a great Data Scientist and take part in the fourth industrial revolution, the information age. For now I'm interested in Natural language processing and data governance, I think there are many things to define in the process of implementing AI in the societies of the world, we must agree and resolve many ethical issues before we are able to implement the AI in our lives in a effective way.
As I like to study, a large part of my dreams have been related to learning, many of them have already been fulfilled, however, many more have not yet. After I finish my university degree I'd like to study a postgraduate degree at one of the best universities in my area, this is so difficult, but I have always thought that with effort and perseverance we can achieve any dream we have. Another of my goals is to work in one of the big technology companies and thus obtain experience in the quality of the process and team work of these companies.
When I work in a team I always look at the soft and technical skills of my co-workers and what they enjoy doing. What satisfies me the most when leading a team is guiding each member to do what they enjoy the most while using their skills to the fullest, it's really satisfying to see a team working excitedly to achieve a common goal.
I want to achieve a leadership position in a company that contributes to the social and economic development of my country, I think it's a great opportunity to be a potential agent of change and thus improve the quality of life of my compatriots. Finally, I keep looking for needs (opportunities) to start my own Start-up and contribute directly to people.
